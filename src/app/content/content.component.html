AWL is a community created out of pure frustration over the anti-cheat absence of Call of Duty: Warzone. It is also a hub to find other passionate players looking for people to play with.

Find & play with new friends

Link up and engage with other Warzone players who are keen to expand their friend circle, and to say goodbye to filling their lobbies with randoms all the time.

Growing a Warzone Community

Growing this community means bringing in other players , so please tell your family and friends about us!

Custom, competitive Warzone lobbies

We are all sick and tired of the cheating problem within Warzone. Our goal is to have whitelisted custom lobbies within the community for some fun but fair rivalry.
